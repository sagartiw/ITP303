CIVIC serves as a community’s search engine, volunteer network, and program calendar all at once. We hope to receive sponsorship from municipalities looking to increase civic engagement in their communities, marketing our product as a consolidated place for local government to provide residents with easy access to information on topics such as elected representatives, local legislative updates, and ongoing social impact projects. Furthermore, the design of our product will propagate private individuals and organizations to act on the information provided by the public sector. By building a profile based on social issue interests and parameters for contribution, we can target specific pathways for individuals to take in order to help solve the problems that they care about. We add the Learn with the Advocate to produce an accurate set of opportunities for one to Participate in.

Bridging the gap between individuals and their community.

CIVIC connects people to causes in their communities. With our innovative LAP process, our platform allows users to Learn about social issues affecting their community, Advocate for certain issues by creating an individualized profile, and Participate in programs and events sponsored by both public and private organizations. By partitioning our product into three discrete channels, we see the potential for an application that can become a staple on every citizen’s phone or computer.
